QwibGift - About: Charity

Ugandan Foot Children's Charity

Save the Orphans

Recently, we, qwibgift, have partnered with the Ugandan Foot Children's Charity in order to help the orphans in Uganda who are not as priveliged as us. In conjunction with this, we have released the Charity tier of our subscription service, where you will recieve a toeless sock with the rest of your gifts. Not only will 75% of the money from the subscription go to the Ugandan Foot Children's Charity, the toes from the socks will also be donated, to help the orphans there stay warm!

How we help...

The Ugandan Foot Children's Charity recieves five cut-off toe pieces for every Charity subscriber every month. As a result, the off-cuts from your socks are used to cover the toes of young Ugandan orphans, keeping them warm throughout these hard months!

Because the sock-toes are so small, unfortunatly, they wear out over time, so some of the Orphans have only four or three. Because the Ugandan orphans have such big hearts, they will donate their two remaining sock-toes so that someone will have a full set - at least someone can be happier! And, after all, thats what Christmas / Halloween is all about!
